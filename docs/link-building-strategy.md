# Link Building & Domain Authority Strategy

## Current State
- Domain: healthinsurancerenew.com
- DR: TBD (run Ahrefs or Moz)
- Target DR: 60+ (required for aggressive indexation of 15M+ URLs)

## The Problem
Google will not deeply crawl a 15M-URL site with DR < 40. The sitemap infrastructure
is correct, but without domain authority, Googlebot allocates minimal crawl budget.

## Strategy 1: Data Journalism (Highest ROI)

We have 15.2M rows of proprietary structured data. No one else has this cross-referenced
at the plan+drug+state level. This is a goldmine for data stories.

### Story angles to pitch:
1. "The 10 Most Expensive States for Diabetes Medication Under ACA Plans"
   - Data: tier distribution + cost ranges for Metformin/Ozempic/Jardiance by state
   - Target: Forbes Health, CNBC, NPR health desk, Kaiser Health News
   - Methodology page on site links back as source

2. "Which ACA Plans Actually Cover Ozempic? A 50-State Analysis"
   - Data: coverage rate, tier, PA requirements across all marketplace plans
   - Target: health reporters covering GLP-1 weight loss drug controversy
   - Timely: GLP-1 drugs are the biggest health story of 2025-2026

3. "The States Where Prior Authorization Blocks the Most Prescriptions"
   - Data: PA rates by state across top 50 drugs
   - Target: patient advocacy outlets, health policy blogs

4. "ACA Subsidy Cliff Returns in 2026 — Who Loses Coverage?"
   - Data: subsidy calculations showing the cliff impact by income bracket + county
   - Target: policy reporters, local news in high-impact states

### Execution:
- Generate the data tables using existing ETL scripts
- Create methodology pages on site (these become link targets)
- Pitch via HARO, Connectively, direct email to health beat reporters
- Timeline: 1 story per month, 12 stories in year 1
- Goal: 20-30 DR60+ backlinks from data journalism alone

## Strategy 2: CMS / Government Citation

- Submit site to relevant .gov resource pages
- Get listed on state exchange "helpful resources" pages
- Apply for NABIP or similar industry association listings
- These are slow but extremely high-trust signals

## Strategy 3: Tool Embeds

- Build embeddable subsidy calculator widget
- Offer free embed to health insurance bloggers
- Each embed = a backlink from their domain
- Target: personal finance blogs, benefits consultants, HR portals

## Timeline
- Month 1-3: Data journalism pitches (stories 1-2)
- Month 3-6: Government citation applications + tool embed launch
- Month 6-12: Sustained monthly data stories + PR
- Month 12+: Evaluate acquisition interest based on traffic + DR

## Success Metrics
- DR 40 by month 6
- DR 60 by month 12
- 100K+ organic monthly visitors by month 9
- 200K+ by month 18 (8-figure trajectory)
